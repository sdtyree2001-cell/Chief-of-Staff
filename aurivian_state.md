## Product Vision (TL;DR)

Aurivian is an **AI-native, Results-as-a-Service (RaaS)** platform that extends Medical Affairs teams into places they cannot occupy — 24/7/365, autonomous agents that replace humans at scale.

**Current phase:** Start with 3 agent types:
- Medical Insights Agent (The Pulse Intelligence Brief)
- KOL Intelligence Agent
- Congress Intelligence Agent

**Future phase:** Chain agents together into a complete Medical Affairs workflow automation system.

**Why we win:**
- All-the-same-shit-for-half-the-price vs. Sorcero
- Data stays in customer environment (vs. Sorcero's hosted model)
- AI-native ops (no humans in the loop) vs. consultancy agencies
- Digital twins of HCPs before first call ("creepy" level of insight)
