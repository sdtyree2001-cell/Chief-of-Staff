## THE PRODUCT VISION (hold this in full)

### The Multiplier Framing

Aurivian is not a tool. It is a MULTIPLIER. It extends reach, presence,
and intelligence-gathering into the hours, places, and interactions the
founder (and his customers' MSL teams) cannot personally occupy.

Three compounding dimensions:

- Always On: no breaks, nights, weekends, vacations
- Always Multiplying: one MSL becomes many; one KOL conversation becomes
  hundreds simulated
- Always Compounding: each interaction makes the next one smarter

### The Five Interaction Modes (roadmap)

1. The Brief — intelligence that arrives (Teams/Slack, 6:30 AM, branded)
2. The Audio Brief — intelligence you listen to (NPR Morning Edition, ~2 min)
3. The Conversational Agent — intelligence you talk to (mobile, spoken)
4. The KOL Simulator — intelligence you rehearse with (digital KOL twin)
5. The Live Agent Call — intelligence you meet with (Zoom/FaceTime, human)

### The Brief Methodology (non-negotiable for any brief we produce)

- External signal ONLY — no customer-owned content, no company websites
- Velocity over volume — what's accelerating, not what's frequent
- Four-community framework: Providers / Patients / Payers / Competitive
- Every section has a Field Validation Question (specific, testable)
- Situation Room written LAST (synthesizes central tension)
- Bottom Line is exactly 4 items, specific and time-bounded
- All sources real, verified, and linked
- Brief branded to customer — feels like theirs, not ours
